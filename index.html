About the story

The four nations are at war. Only the Avatar can unite the world and end the war.The young Avatar has already mastered the three elements and has to find a firebending master, but the fire nation does not allow outsiders anymore . The Avatar must a find a way inside the fire nation alone in order to find a wise firebending master called Shirokahn
