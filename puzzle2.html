Second -Task

The Avatar has entered the fire nation but lost the way. The Avatar is lost in the Fire Nation and needs to find the way to the Fire Bending Master. They come across a signpost with the following message:

To find the Fire Bending Master, you mustadd the number of flames on the left torch with the number of flames on the right torch. Then, subtract the number of flames on the center torch. The result is the number of steps you must take to reach the Fire Bending Master. The left torch has 6 flames, the center torch has 2 flames, and the right torch has 3 flames. How many steps should the Avatar take
