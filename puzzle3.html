Third -Task

After finding his way to master Shirokahn, the avatar must prove himself by solving a problem given by the master. This problem is a prerequisite for the master to accept the avatar as his disciple. The avatar must demonstrate his skill and intelligence to earn the trust of the master.

Master Shirokahn presents the Avatar with an ethical dilemma as part of the acceptance process for becoming his disciple. He asks the Avatar to choose between saving the life of an innocent person or saving the lives of many people. The Avatar must make a tough decision and explain their reasoning to the master.

Save the life of One Save the life of many
